Memberships

We have 3 differnet memberships available that give you different benefits in the gym.

  • Purple Card Member $20/Month: This is the basic membership you get when joing E.L. Fitness. You have access to all equipment but If you wish to take a class, you must pay a seperate $30 fee .

  • Red Card Member $30/Month: This is the mid tier level membership. You have access to all eqipment, you are now allowed to sign in guests, and class fees drop to $12.

  • Black Card Member $50/Month: The highest membership level thath comes with all previous level benefits but classes are free and you have access to online personal trainers to help with dieting and workouts for each week.
